ABOUT

An International Workshop on Building Humanitarian Decision-Making Tools in the Age of Big Data

An invite-only international workshop hosted by the Dahdaleh Institute for Global Health Research (DIGHR) and the Lassonde School of Engineering (LSE) at York University that brings together leading humanitarian agencies, data scientists, and developers to collaborate on urgent data-related challenges in humanitarian response.



Challenges

WASH Data Portal

Global Nutrition Dashboard

Cash Delivery Data

NLP for Remote Needs Assessments

Water Optimization Tool



WHO IS COMING?

We will bring together participants from five key groups needed to solve complex humanitarian data science challenges: Humanitarian Field & Subject Matter Experts, Humanitarian Innovation Specialists, Data and Computer Science Researchers, Tech Developers, Ethicists & Governance Specialists.
